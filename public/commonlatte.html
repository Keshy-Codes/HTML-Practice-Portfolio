Most Common Latte Flavors

Based on my observations only

Caramel Machiatto

I think no cafe that has none of this flavor,a staple for Filipino taste indeed!

Classic Iced Latte/ Spanish Latte

This is two different flavors but I put as one as I'm not fan of both; the classic is mild flavor while the Spanish is too milky flavor.

Mocha Latte

For the chocolate lovers out there. However, not all cafes have greate chocolatey taste
